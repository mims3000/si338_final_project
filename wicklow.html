Wicklow

First solo trip outside of Dublin! I had such a fun time looking at some castle ruins along my hike. The town was calm since I arrived later in the afternoon. I just spent my time there very slowly — it was very cathartic. I strongly recommend coming to this area, it does not disappoint!
