Killruddery House & Gardens

The Killruddery House and Gardens are located on an 800-acre estate of the Brabazon family and have evolved with each generation. Today, the enormous property features various food spots, landscaped gardens, farmland, and a shop.

Interesting vibes. Beautiful scenery.

Dreamlike place, but the house and gardens are not very physically accessible given the stone flooring, gravel pathways, tree roots, staircases, and lack of elevators and Braille. Additionally, there were multiple private or no access signs around the property which made the atmosphere feel a bit exclusive.
