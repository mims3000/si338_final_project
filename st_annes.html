Impromptu visit. Ended up being a longer stay.

Fun pit stop after a class visit to the Irish Wheelchair Association (IWA). Arden and I walked to the nearby St. Anne's Park and had a photoshoot on the Annie Lee Tower Bridge. Apparently, it is a sham ruin that was constructed in 1839 by Benjamin Lee Guinness to celebrate his daughter Annie Lee's birth!
