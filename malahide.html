Pretty town. Went twice.

May 8 and 9, 2026: I had so much fun, I had to go twice. Just kidding! On Friday, May 8, my classmate Arden and I arrived to Malahide once the castle and gardens were closing. We walked around the park grounds and along the shoreline instead. The following morning, I went back with my roommate Tasnimah and we did the castle tour! We especially loved the stream in the "Dragon's Den" along the fairy trail.
