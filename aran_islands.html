Aran Islands

Multimodal transportation. Rocky in multiple ways.

The weekend of our class trip to Galway, Olivia, Tasnimah, Arden, and I went across the way to the Aran Islands. Getting around entailed a ferry ride and bike rentals, andw e specifically went to Inis Mór. I hiked towards Dún Aonghasa, a prehistoric hill fort, and the view of the water from the highest cliff was amazing! The spots of aqua blue were mesmerizing even though I was a bit scared to get too close to the edge.
