Cliffs of Moher

What a time! I was really looking forward to this weekend trip, and seeing what the Aran Islands were already like got me even more excited.

Gloomy weather, yet still a wonderful view

I honestly like the rain and gloomy weather (mostly when I am in indoors, but the bus ride was worth it)! The infamous cliffs live up to their name, but I would strongly recommend venturing to other trails and hikes beyond these ones in Galway!
