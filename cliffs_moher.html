Cliffs of Moher

May 8 and 9, 2026: I had so much fun, I had to go twice. Just kidding! On Friday, May 8, my classmate Arden and I arrived to Malahide once the castle and gardens were closing. So, I would suggest checking the opening hours and guided tour times in advance. We proceeded to walk around the lush green park grounds and along the shoreline instead.

Pretty town. Went twice.

The following morning, I went back with my roommate Tasnimah, and we did both the gardens and castle tour! The gardens were so whimsical and enchanting. We especially loved the stream in the "Dragon's Den" along the fairy trail. Additionally, the castle tour was quite interesting and short if you are not looking for anything too lengthy.
